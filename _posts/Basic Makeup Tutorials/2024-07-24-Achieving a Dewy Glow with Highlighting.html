Introduction

Highlighting is a makeup technique used to emphasize certain features on your face, giving it a radiant, youthful glow. Achieving a dewy finish is particularly popular because it imparts a fresh, healthy look. In this tutorial, we will delve into the basics of highlighting, providing a detailed guide on how to achieve a dewy glow.

What is Highlighting?

Highlighting involves applying a lighter shade of makeup to specific areas of the face that naturally catch the light. This technique enhances your facial structure and creates an illuminated, vibrant appearance. Common areas for highlighting include the tops of the cheekbones, the bridge of the nose, the brow bone, and the cupid's bow.

Prepping Your Skin

For a flawless highlight, it's essential to prepare your skin properly. This involves cleansing, moisturizing, and priming your face to create a smooth canvas for makeup application.

Cleansing

Start with a gentle cleanser to remove any dirt, oil, or makeup residue. Clean skin ensures better adherence of products and prevents clogged pores.

Moisturizing

Apply a hydrating moisturizer suited to your skin type. Well-moisturized skin helps makeup blend seamlessly and prevents dry patches.

Priming

Use a primer to even out your skin texture and prolong the wear of your makeup. Choose a primer that offers a luminous finish to complement the dewy look.

Choosing the Right Products

To achieve a dewy glow, selecting the appropriate products is crucial. Here are some product recommendations:

Highlighters

Opt for highlighters that offer a natural sheen without chunky glitter. Liquid and cream highlighters are excellent for a dewy finish as they blend seamlessly into the skin.

Foundation

Choose a lightweight, luminous foundation that enhances your skin's natural radiance. Avoid matte formulas, as they can diminish the dewy effect.

Setting Sprays

Finish your makeup with a hydrating setting spray to lock in moisture and add an extra layer of luminosity.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Apply Foundation

Start with a luminous foundation, applying it evenly across your face using a brush, sponge, or your fingers. Blend well to ensure there are no harsh lines.

Step 2: Conceal

Use a concealer to cover any blemishes or dark circles. Blend it out seamlessly with your foundation.

Step 3: Highlight

Apply your chosen highlighter to the high points of your face:

  • Cheekbones: Sweep the highlighter on the tops of your cheekbones, blending upwards towards your temples.
  • Bridge of the Nose: Lightly apply down the bridge of your nose for a subtle glow.
  • Brow Bone: Add a touch under the arch of your brows to lift and brighten the area.
  • Cupid's Bow: Dab a small amount on your cupid's bow to enhance your lip shape.

Step 4: Blend

Use a damp makeup sponge or a blending brush to seamlessly blend the highlighter into your skin. This step ensures a natural, dewy finish without harsh lines.

Step 5: Set

Lightly dust a translucent powder on areas prone to oiliness, such as the T-zone. Avoid over-powdering as it can reduce the dewy effect.

Step 6: Finishing Touch

Spritz a hydrating setting spray all over your face to set the makeup and enhance the glow.

Tips and Tricks

  • Layering: For a more intense glow, layer a powder highlighter over a liquid or cream highlighter.
  • Natural Light: Always check your makeup in natural light to ensure it looks blended and seamless.
  • Skin Type: Adjust your product choices based on your skin type. Oily skin may benefit from lighter, gel-based highlighters, while dry skin can use creamier products.
  • Subtle Glow: For a subtle glow, mix a few drops of liquid highlighter into your foundation before application.
  • Tools: Use the right tools for application. Brushes work well for precise placement, while sponges are great for blending.

Conclusion

Highlighting is an effective technique to achieve a dewy, radiant glow that enhances your natural beauty. By following the steps and tips outlined in this guide, you can master the art of highlighting and create a fresh, luminous look perfect for any occasion.
